Spaghetti Bolognese

Ingredients:

  • 400g Spaghetti
  • 500g Ground beef
  • 1 Onion, chopped
  • 2 Garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 can of Tomatoes
  • 1 tbsp Olive oil
  • Salt & pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Boil the spaghetti according to package instructions.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan, add onion and garlic, and cook until softened.
  3. Add ground beef and cook until browned.
  4. Pour in the canned tomatoes, season with salt and pepper, and simmer for 20 minutes.
  5. Serve the sauce over the cooked spaghetti. Garnish with fresh basil if desired.

Stir-Fry

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Broccoli
  • 1 Red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 Carrot, julienned
  • 200g Chicken breast, thinly sliced
  • 2 tbsp Soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp Sesame oil
  • 1 tbsp Ginger, grated
  • 2 Garlic cloves, minced

Instructions:

  1. Heat sesame oil in a pan over medium heat.
  2. Add ginger and garlic, cook for 1 minute.
  3. Add chicken and cook until browned.
  4. Add vegetables and stir-fry for 5-7 minutes.
  5. Add soy sauce, stir, and cook for another 2 minutes. Serve hot.

Chocolate Cake

Ingredients:

  • 200g Flour
  • 150g Sugar
  • 100g Cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp Baking powder
  • 2 Eggs
  • 100ml Milk
  • 100g Butter
  • 1 tsp Vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Mix dry ingredients in a bowl: flour, cocoa, sugar, baking powder.
  3. Add eggs, milk, and melted butter. Stir until smooth.
  4. Pour the batter into a greased cake pan and bake for 30-35 minutes.
  5. Allow to cool, then frost if desired. Serve and enjoy!
