Bugatti Chiron

The Bugatti Chiron is a marvel of engineering and luxury. With breathtaking speed and an iconic design, it stands as a symbol of automotive excellence.

Top Features

  • 8.0L Quad-Turbocharged W16 Engine
  • Maximum Speed: 261 mph (electronically limited)
  • Horsepower: 1,500 hp
  • 0 to 60 mph in under 2.4 seconds
  • Luxury leather interiors with customizable options

Interesting Facts

  • The Chiron's top speed exceeds 300 mph in a special edition model.
  • Each Bugatti Chiron takes several months to handcraft.
  • Its brake system uses carbon-ceramic discs for superior performance.
  • The price of a Bugatti Chiron starts at $3 million.
